# FreshKart Sales Performance Analysis — Accio-ISB Collaboration

## Overview
Analyzed monthly sales data for FreshKart across 12 Indian cities
and 4 product categories, as part of the Accio-ISB Bangalore
data analytics initiative.

## Assignment Structure
- **Q4 & Q5** cover the same problem with slightly different approaches
- Q4 includes contribution margin analysis in the What-If section
- Q5 includes an additional cross-tab pivot (month × product category)

## What Was Done
- Built KPI sheets: total revenue, avg price, sales per customer
- Created pivot tables by month, city, product category
- Simulated a 10% smartphone discount for March, June, September
- Modeled 3 business scenarios: Growth, Conservative, Downturn

## Key Findings
- Total annual revenue: ₹63.6 Crores
- Surat and Pune are top-performing cities
- Smartphones = 50% of total revenue
- A 10% discount on smartphones causes ~₹34.5L profit loss
- Growth scenario (20% volume + 5% price increase) yields best returns
